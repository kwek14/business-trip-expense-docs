# 출장비 증빙 분류 규칙

Use these rules when deciding whether a trip cost can be placed in the reimbursement total.

## General Evidence Standard

An amount may be included in `정산 요청 금액` only when the package can show:

- what was purchased or charged,
- who or what trip it relates to,
- service date or usage period,
- merchant or issuer,
- amount and currency,
- payment or settlement evidence, and
- no unresolved refund or duplicate-claim issue.

If one element is missing, classify the line as `청구 가능 후보` or `추가 확인 필요`, not as confirmed.

## Cost-Type Matrix

| Cost type | Usually required evidence | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 숙박비 | booking confirmation or hotel folio, receipt/invoice, card approval or payment statement, stay dates | Split bookings may be grouped narratively but keep separate financial rows. |
| 항공료 | e-ticket or itinerary receipt, payment receipt/card approval, ticket number or booking reference | Boarding passes are helpful when institution requires travel proof. |
| 학회등록비 | conference registration invoice/receipt, payment proof, event name/date | Include accepted paper or attendance evidence only if requested. |
| 현지교통비 | ticket/receipt, ride receipt, card approval when available | Route/date should match trip scope. |
| 관광세/도시세/현지세 | hotel folio, local tax receipt, booking page showing taxes not included, or final settlement receipt | Keep separate from room rate unless source explicitly combines them. |
| 환불/취소 | original charge evidence plus refund/cancellation transaction or merchant notice | Claim net amount only when both sides are known. |
| 개인카드 선결제 | merchant receipt plus personal-card approval/statement | Cardholder identity and payment amount should be visible enough for review. |

## Deposits and Preauthorizations

Classify as `가승인/보증금` when evidence says or implies:

- preauthorization,
- deposit,
- security hold,
- temporary/provisional ticket,
- initialisation pre-autorisation,
- estimated amount,
- pending refund, or
- card approval whose final expense purpose is not yet documented.

Do not include the full deposit in reimbursement totals. If a tax or damage charge is deducted from the deposit, claim only the finally retained amount after obtaining a hotel folio, final receipt, refund transaction, or written merchant confirmation.

## Local Taxes and Fees

- `Taxes not included`, `local tax`, `city tax`, `tourist tax`, `taxe de sejour`, or similar wording means the tax is separate from the booking room rate.
- If local tax was paid at the hotel, create a separate line.
- If local tax will be deducted from a deposit, classify the deposit as `환불대기` and the tax as `청구 가능 후보` until the final retained amount is documented.
- Never describe a one-night lodging payment as including tourist tax unless the source explicitly says the tax was included in that amount.

## Card Approval vs Final Settlement

Korean card app screenshots are useful evidence when they show merchant, KRW amount, transaction date, card, approval number, and status.

However:

- A normal approval can still later be cancelled or partially refunded.
- If the user says a refund is pending, mark the amount as `환불대기` or show it outside the requested total until final settlement evidence exists.
- If multiple card approvals occur minutes apart at the same hotel, reconcile them against folios, deposits, taxes, and booking amounts before assigning categories.

## Currency and KRW Amounts

- If a Korean card charge is visible in KRW, use the KRW card amount for the reimbursement line.
- If only foreign currency is visible, include source currency and note that KRW conversion requires the institution's exchange-rate rule or card settlement statement.
- Do not invent exchange rates. Use a source-backed rate only when supplied or when explicitly requested to look up the applicable institutional standard.

## Conflict Handling

When evidence conflicts:

- Keep the conflict visible in the package.
- Use `추가 확인 필요` for the affected line.
- State what evidence would resolve it, such as final hotel folio, cancellation receipt, refund approval, card statement, or booking platform e-receipt.
